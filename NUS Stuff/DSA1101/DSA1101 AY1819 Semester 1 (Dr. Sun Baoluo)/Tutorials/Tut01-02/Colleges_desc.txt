Description: These data were collected from the 25 top liberal arts colleges and the 25 top research
universities. There are 50 cases on 8 variables.

Number of cases: 50
Variable Names:
School: Contains the name of each school
School_Type: Coded 'LibArts' for liberal arts and 'Univ' for university
SAT: Median combined Math and Verbal SAT score of students
Acceptance: % of applicants accepted
DPerStudent: Money spent per student in dollars
Top 10p: % of students in the top 10% of their h.s. graduating class
PerPhD: % of faculty at the institution that have PhD degrees
GradPer: % of students at institution who eventually graduate
